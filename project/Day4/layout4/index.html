INDORE: Private schools and business establishments in the city will be closed on Thursday till afternoon in view of Bharat Bandh call given by several organisations in protest against amendments in SC/ST (Prevention of Atrocities) Act. “Following the call of nationwide protest, 40 social organisations have extended their support. Markets in city including Siyaganj, Cloth Market, Sarafa, Bartan Bazaar, Timber and Dawa Bazaar have announced to keep their establishments close till 2pm,” Samanya, Pichhda Evam Alpsankhyak Warg Adhikari Karmachari Sanstha (SPAKAS) district president Jagdish Joshi told TOI.
