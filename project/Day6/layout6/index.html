Usefulness of cats

In our urban and suburban houses what should we do without cats? In our sitting or bedroom, our libraries, in our kitchen and storerooms, our farms, barns, and rickyars, in our docks, our granaries, our ships , and our wharves, in our corn markets, meat markets,and other places tooo numerous to mention,how usefill they are in our ships, the rats off set them at defiance still they are of great service
